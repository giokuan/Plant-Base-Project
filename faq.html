FAQs

Shopping

Do your plants come in pots?

All our plants come in pots. If you wish to add a special pot, you can message at and we will handle your request.

Is there a minimum order purchase?

For our personal deliveries, we have a minimum purchase of at least ₱500. If you wish to opt for a third-party courier, you may do so. But please be advised that our team won't be liable for any damages that may occur in transit.

How do I place an order?

Add your desired items into your cart by clicking the cart icon button. As soon as you are ready to check out, click the "Cart" button in the upper right part of the website. Here, you can update/change/add items in your shopping cart, you may add a discount code if you have one. The following step is checking out, by placing your address and payment information and confirming the order.

Payment

Do you accept Cash on Delivery Method?

Yes we accept Cash on Delivery Method.

Do you have discounts?

Yes. You can use the voucher ticket available. New buyers can also enjoy ₱100 discount on their first purchase.

What is your mode of payments?

We accept Cash On Delivery, GCash, and through online bank transfer.

Shipping

What is your delivery schedule?

We deliver during weekends. Once you successfully place an order, we'll prepare and ship it by friday night.

Can I have my plants picked up instead of door to door delivery?

Yes. There is an option in the checkout page where you can choose between pick up or door to door delivery. Just choose what is your desirable way of delivery.

How long does it take to receive my plants?

It depends on where you are residing. For NCR buyers, it would take 1-2 days and in provinces, maximum is 1 week.

Others

What plants can you recommend for beginners?

Golden Pothos, Sansevieria and Aglaonema are beginner-friendly plants since they can easily maintained and not sensitive plants.

What plants are safe for pets and babies?

Christmas Cactus, Boston Fern, African Violet, Peperomia are some of the safe plants to have when you have pets or babies.

Do you accept bulk orders?

Yes we do! Just provide complete contact information so we can arrange your orders and schedule a faster way. The more plants you order, the more is the discount!
