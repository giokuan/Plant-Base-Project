About Us

PlantBase offers a wide variety of ornamental plants that are great for adding flavor to your indoor or outdoor design taste. We provide different categories and types of plants that suits for what you need. If you are looking for a fresh and affordable plants, this is the perfect place for you.

Based in the Philippines, we started as a small business and hobby and as time flies, we decided to make it through online platform. PlantBase aims to sparkled a global movement in promoting environmental awareness towards a cleaner and greener planet by engaging people into planting and appreciate nature.

Front Door Delivery

We have our own logistic team that handles front door delivery. Rest assured that all plants are delivered safe in front of your door.

Affordable Plants

Low prices of plants offered with guaranteed premium quality.

Multiple Payment Methods

Customers have multiple options available for secured payment methods. We accept GCash, credit/debit card and cash on delivery options.
